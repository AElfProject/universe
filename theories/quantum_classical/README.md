# 量子宇宙-量子观察者-经典意识论 | Quantum Universe-Quantum Observer-Classical Consciousness Theory

## 概述 | Overview

量子宇宙-量子观察者-经典意识论（QUQOCC理论）是一种尝试整合量子物理学与意识研究的理论框架，探索量子力学、观测过程与意识现象之间的可能联系。该理论提出宇宙基本上是量子性的，观察者同样受量子规律支配，而意识则可能是在量子-经典边界上涌现的现象，在测量过程中扮演特殊角色。这一理论框架仍处于探索阶段，结合了物理学、认知科学和哲学的元素，试图为量子测量问题、意识的物理基础以及主观经验与客观实在的关系提供新的视角。

The Quantum Universe-Quantum Observer-Classical Consciousness Theory (QUQOCC Theory) is a theoretical framework attempting to integrate quantum physics with consciousness studies, exploring possible connections between quantum mechanics, observation processes, and consciousness phenomena. The theory proposes that the universe is fundamentally quantum in nature, observers are likewise governed by quantum laws, while consciousness may be a phenomenon emerging at the quantum-classical boundary, playing a special role in the measurement process. This theoretical framework remains in the exploratory stage, combining elements from physics, cognitive science, and philosophy, attempting to provide new perspectives on the quantum measurement problem, the physical basis of consciousness, and the relationship between subjective experience and objective reality.
