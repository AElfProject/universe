# 量子宇宙 | Quantum Universe

## 概述 | Overview

量子宇宙理论提出宇宙在其最基本层面是量子力学的，整个宇宙可以被描述为一个巨大的量子系统或波函数。这一理念源于将量子力学原理应用于宇宙学，试图解决经典宇宙学和广义相对论在极端条件下（如宇宙起源、黑洞中心或普朗克尺度）的局限性。量子宇宙观念挑战了我们对实在、时间和因果关系的传统理解，提出宇宙可能具有本质上的不确定性、非局域性和叠加态特性，这些特性在宏观尺度上通过退相干机制表现为我们熟悉的经典宇宙。

The Quantum Universe theory proposes that the universe at its most fundamental level is quantum mechanical, with the entire universe describable as a vast quantum system or wavefunction. This idea originates from applying quantum mechanics principles to cosmology, attempting to resolve limitations of classical cosmology and general relativity under extreme conditions (such as universe origin, black hole centers, or Planck scale). The quantum universe concept challenges our traditional understanding of reality, time, and causality, suggesting the universe may possess inherent uncertainty, non-locality, and superposition characteristics, which manifest through decoherence mechanisms as the familiar classical universe at macroscopic scales.
